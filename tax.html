What is the Quebec Tax Calculator?

This free Quebec tax calculator helps you calculate GST/TPS and QST/TVQ on any price. You can calculate the total after tax or reverse-calculate the price before tax.

Quebec sales tax rates

In Quebec, GST/TPS is calculated at 5% and QST/TVQ is calculated at 9.975%. Together, they add up to 14.975%.

How to use this calculator

  • Select “Before Tax” if you want to add tax to a price.
  • Select “After Tax” if you want to find the price before tax.
  • Enter the amount and the result will update instantly.
