What is the Quebec Tax Calculator?

This free Quebec tax calculator helps you calculate GST/TPS and QST/TVQ on any price. Enter the price before tax and the tool will show the GST/TPS amount, the QST/TVQ amount, and the total price after tax.

Quebec sales tax rates

In Quebec, GST/TPS is calculated at 5% and QST/TVQ is calculated at 9.975%. Together, they are commonly used to calculate sales tax on many purchases in Quebec.

How to use this calculator

  • Enter the price before tax.
  • The calculator will add GST/TPS and QST/TVQ.
  • You will instantly see the total price after tax.
