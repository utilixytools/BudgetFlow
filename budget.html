What is BudgetFlow?

BudgetFlow is a free online income and expense tracker. It helps you calculate your total income, subtract your monthly expenses, and see how much money you have left.

Who can use this budget tracker?

This tool is useful for students, workers, freelancers, and anyone who wants to manage their monthly budget more easily.

How to use BudgetFlow

  • Enter your income sources.
  • Enter your monthly expenses.
  • Click calculate to see your money left.
